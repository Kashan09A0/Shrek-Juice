Franchising Opportunity

The best low-cost franchising opportunity – We hope you will join us!

Find out how to make your entrepreneurial dream come true now.

With Shrek-Juice Franchising, you can start your own business of healthy street food, offering customers healthy, innovative, organic and fresh products.

The company was founded by an entrepreneur who for over 30 years has been working in the fruit and vegetable sale and production industry, and thanks to his experience, he’s been able to keep up with the market evolution and customers’ needs.

Get Started

If you love to get involved, if you want to make a significant improvement in your life, change and grow personally and professionally, become a successful entrepreneur, the protagonist of your life, do not miss this unique opportunity, join us in this project.

We offer a training course to the affiliate to deepen the philosophy and know-how of the brand and at the same time learning about the different products we offer.

Administration and Performance Policy

Before and during the activity, free training courses will be organized.

The facilities preparation and decoration, either of corners or vehicles, has to respect the company’s policy.
The enterprise administration doesn’t include minimumorder volumes; the merchandise is restocked according todaily needs, especially fruit and vegetables, asperishable goods, to protect their freshness.
From time to time offers will be made on the items,especially on seasonal fruit and vegetables.

The goods’ conditions have to be controlled three times: in the country of origin before shipping, once arrived in Malta’s warehouses and at the end once delivered to the affiliate. If the above-mentioned conditions don’t respect the freshness standards established by the parent company, the products have to be reported and sent back.

Cleaning and Sanitization Policy

Shrek-Juice provides aprons, hats and other gadgets of the brand for free.

Blenders/mixers have to be cleaned after each use to not affect the taste of the different juices.

Areas must be cleaned and sanitized every day.

Working Hours & Prices

Working hours are preferably from 7:30 AM to 9:00 PM, since we sell a product that can be consumed at any time.

A predetermined price list must be respected. Eventual changes on the above mentioned must be discussed with the parent company.

For the inauguration of new sales points,Shrek-Juice offers free juices and tastes of fruit and vegetables to customers to promote and test the product.
